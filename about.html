About Us

Welcome to Neon Nexus Café, Sweden's first 24/7 gaming café with three locations! We're dedicated to providing a top-notch gaming experience in a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Below is more information regarding our services, locations to keep our customers informed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Age Restrictions

All gamers of all ages are welcome to our facilities during 10:00-18:00. If you are under the age of 13, you must be accompanied by an adult after 18:00. The games available for minors under 13 will also be restricted. This is to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone. Minors must get a parents consent before signing up for an account.

How can I book a gaming session?

You can book a gaming session through our website, app or by visiting one of our locations. Please visit our locations page for more information regarding prices.

Do you serve food and drinks?

Yes, we have a café menu with a selection of warm food, snacks and beverages to keep you fueled during your gaming sessions. Alcoholic beverages are also available for our adult guests between 10:00-24:00. Order at the counter or through our app to have it delivered straight to your gaming station!

What games are available?

We offer a wide range of popular games across various genres, including FPS, MOBA, RPG, and more. Our game library is regularly updated to include the latest releases and classics. Please note that some games may have age restrictions based on their ratings.

What equipment do you provide?

Our gaming stations are equipped with high-performance PCs, gaming consoles, and accessories such as headsets, keyboards, and mice. We also offer VR setups for an immersive gaming experience. If you have specific equipment preferences, please let us know when booking.

Have specific questions about a gaming center?

Please visit our contact page to get in touch with us directly.
